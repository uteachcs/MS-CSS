## Lesson Description
This lesson introduces social engineering attacks.

## Activities & Pacing 
_*The reading and activities in this assignment can be completed asynchronously._

### Social Engineering (25 min.)
1. Direct students to the **Configuring Wireless Network Security** assignment.
1. Students read the following lesson pages and complete the assessment questions:
	- Configuring Wireless Network Security
	- Wireless Access Points
	- Controlling Wireless Signal Range
	- Wireless Encryption
	- Controlling Who Can Connect
1. Encourage students to focus on how each configuration choice helps reduce security risks.

### Social Engineering Activities (25 min.)
1. Direct students to the **Social Engineering Activities** page.
1. Students complete the activities and assessments on ways to gather data for reconnaissance.
1. Conclude the lesson with the discussion prompts in the **Teacher Guidance** section.

## Teacher Preparation
1. Read through the student pages and activities for reference.
	
### Instructional Approaches
- Discussion, whole class
- Exploration and response, individual
